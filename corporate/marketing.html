About us

Rolecall is a creative hub built for the next generation of theatre makers in the Netherlands. Whether you're an actor, director, or writer, this is your stage to grow, connect, and be seen. We believe that every artist—no matter their background, experience, or training - deserves a real shot at the spotlight. That’s why we created Rolecall - a platform where opportunity meets community, and talent meets the right connections. Whether you're looking for open auditions, want to collaborate with others, or need a place to showcase your work, Rolecall gives you the visibility and tools to move your career forward. Rolecall isn’t just a platform - it’s a movement to empower local theatre communities, connect emerging talent, and make the performing arts more open, accessible, and human.

What we offer

Role finder

Find local casting calls in your area and quickly apply. We consolidate opportunities from independent productions, theaters, and drama groups in one location.

Resources & Growth tools

Advice and behind-the-scenes tips from professionals in the field to help you develop your skills and confidently navigate the field.

Portfolio showcase

To upload your resume, headshots, reels, and previous performances, create a personal profile. Casting directors will find you if you let your acting abilities speak for themselves.

Creative networking

Make connections with other theater groups, directors, writers, and actors. Rolecall makes it simple to expand your creative network, whether your goal is to work together, practice, or simply exchange ideas.

Casting board for Theaters & Clubs

A platform designed for the local scene, it allows local theaters, drama schools, and independent productions to post casting calls, browse talent, and get in touch with actors directly.
