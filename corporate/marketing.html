CONTEXT OF CAMPAIGN AND PROMOTIONAL ACTIVITIES

About us

Rolecall is a creative hub built for the next generation of theatre makers in the Netherlands. Whether you're an actor, director, or writer, this is your stage to grow, connect, and be seen. We believe that every artist—no matter their background, experience, or training - deserves a real shot at the spotlight. That’s why we created Rolecall - a platform where opportunity meets community, and talent meets the right connections. Whether you're looking for open auditions, want to collaborate with others, or need a place to showcase your work, Rolecall gives you the visibility and tools to move your career forward. Rolecall isn’t just a platform - it’s a movement to empower local theatre communities, connect emerging talent, and make the performing arts more open, accessible, and human.

What we offer

Role finder

Find local casting calls in your area and quickly apply. We consolidate opportunities from independent productions, theaters, and drama groups in one location.

Resources & Growth tools

Advice and behind-the-scenes tips from professionals in the field to help you develop your skills and confidently navigate the field.

Portfolio showcase

To upload your resume, headshots, reels, and previous performances, create a personal profile. Casting directors will find you if you let your acting abilities speak for themselves.

Creative networking

Make connections with other theater groups, directors, writers, and actors. Rolecall makes it simple to expand your creative network, whether your goal is to work together, practice, or simply exchange ideas.

Casting board for Theaters & Clubs

A platform designed for the local scene, it allows local theaters, drama schools, and independent productions to post casting calls, browse talent, and get in touch with actors directly.

Personas

Social media choice

Our most important users are 18-30-year-old aspiring actors and theatre fans - a demographic that’s highly active on Instagram, with over 51% of Dutch Instagram users falling within the 18-34 age group (Statista, 2024). TikTok follows closely, with roughly half of our interviewees using it regularly. These platforms are where our audience already connects, gets inspired, and discovers new creative opportunities. As visually rich and performance-friendly platforms, Instagram and TikTok allow us to engage with our audience in an organic, entertaining, and authentic way. Whether it's audition tips, actor spotlights, portfolio reels, or backstage moments, our content strategy is designed to: • Build a community around shared passion and creativity • Drive engagement through trends, theatre-related challenges, and interactive content • Highlight users and open casting calls to boost visibility • Attract new talent organically through strong algorithmic reach By showing up where our audience already lives online, we make Rolecall part of their daily scroll - and their journey to the spotlight.

Objectives

Reach (Communication + Measurable + Achievable)

By April 6th, 2025, reach 300 accounts. According to research, people create opinions about a brand based on its messaging, visibility, and consistency even before they interact with it directly (Edelman Trust Barometer, 2023). Reaching 300 people both on Instagram and TikTok is a good place to start when building credibility, particularly in the early stages of brand development. It enables us to establish a feeling of presence, test content strategies, and raise awareness. An active following of a few hundred people can create the groundwork for sustained growth and brand loyalty, according to Kevin Kelly's 1,000 True Fans concept.

Affect (Communication + Measurable)

We want to connect and inspire aspiring actors and creatives who are looking for community and guidance. Our goal is for our content to inspire people to consider and feel encouraged to pursue their creative endeavors. We want to promote deeper engagement through regular posting, value-driven content, and interactive Stories, whether it be through comments, shares, or platform participation. We think we can build a community by the campaign's conclusion that not only supports but also feels a strong emotional bond with our mission and brand.

Response (Communication + Measurable + Achievable)

An average of 300 views on Instagram Reels. • An average of 250 views on TikTok. • An average of 300 views on Instagram Feed. • An average of 10 likes on Instagram Reels and posts. • Over 300 accounts reached on Instagram. • An average of 1 comment on each post. According to Metricool (2025), there isn't an official limit to how many comments a brand can get, but spammy or excessive commenting activity can warrant temporary restrictions. It is preferable to focus on quality, authentic interactions rather than attempting to optimize for quantity.

Campaign

We’re introducing Rolecall as a bold and inclusive creative hub for a new generation of theatre and acting talent. Through our first posts, we define who we are, what we stand for, and what sets us apart. By sharing our mission, vision, and team members, we build trust, rapport, and emotional investment with our audience. These posts humanize the brand and demonstrate that Rolecall is a space created by creatives for creatives. To help us reach our goal of 300 accounts by April 6th, we’re relying on highly visible formats like reels and TikToks. Our educational content, like audition tips and behind-the-scenes clips, offers value to emerging artists while increasing our discoverability. These pieces not only drive views, but also position Rolecall as a resourceful and accessible platform for creatives of all levels. Instagram Stories play a strategic role in driving engagement and community. Through interactive elements such as polls and Q&A, we create a space for followers to share their desires and engage in meaningful dialogue. This helps turn passive viewers into active participants. As the campaign progresses, we introduce content related to the upcoming launch of our website and use teasers to build curiosity and anticipation. These updates reinforce the legitimacy of our platform and show that Rolecall is visionary and practical in what it offers. To maximize attention and deepen engagement, we write longer captions that include personal stories, insights or calls to action. We also use targeted hashtags in all posts and reels to increase visibility within the theatre and creative communities in the Netherlands. Overall, this campaign is not just about visibility – it is about building belonging. Rolecall aims to be more than just a platform. We are launching a movement that amplifies unheard voices, encourages bold creativity, and invites the next wave of theater artists to step into the spotlight.

Content calendar

Initially, we focused on making a good first impression by posting several times in a short period of time. Between March 24 and 29, we posted three types of content on Instagram: a Feed, Stories, Reels, and TikTok. This gave us credibility and ensured that the page wasn't empty when new users discovered our page. Through a combination of posts - from brand and team introductions to audition tips - we tried to engage and bring the essence of our brand to life in a hurry. After the first wave, we switched to posting fairly evenly throughout the week starting in April. By making this adjustment, we've managed to retain existing followers while still attracting new ones. On April 9, we posted a behind-the-scenes video of the show at lunchtime and then developed a scheduling plan based on engagement patterns. On April 14, we also tested the timing of the posts - we moved the content drops in the later hours to the evening time between 6:00 and 7:00 pm. These later posts had significantly better reach and engagement, so we set this time as the time to post later content. In addition to reinforcing branding efforts, we also placed Reels and TikToks from different times in between to keep viewers as engaged as possible over time. We also posted teasers before the site launched to generate buzz. On 16 and 18 April, we used Instagram posts and stories to highlight key features and announce that the site was live. To ensure high engagement, on April 7 we added interactive tools such as Q&A that were clearly visible within the post. These steps played a key role in keeping our audience engaged and invested in our progress.
