JUSTIFICATION OF LEAN CANVAS

Product

Rolecall is a creative hub built for the next generation of theatre makers in the Netherlands. Whether you're an actor, director, or writer, this is your stage to grow, connect, and be seen.

Problem

Difficult to find auditions in one place

Aspiring actors often struggle to find auditions, especially without an agent or agency. Most amateur actors have to rely on paid websites like Backstage to find roles, and even then, they may not be qualified, in the area, or it may not be a role that fits them. This leads to hours of wasted time and missed opportunities. The current landscape is fragmented, with casting calls scattered across social media, forums, or hidden behind expensive paywalls. Role Call solves this by centralizing auditions in one platform, making it accessible and inclusive for all.

Lack of personalized filters

One of the biggest issues that actors face is finding auditions, let alone booking the role. Pre-existing casting websites may only go as far as to specify your age and gender, barely focusing on the true needs and desires of those on the prowl for a job. Every actor is unique — from their niche (e.g., musical theater vs. experimental drama) to their skill set, schedule, and career goals. Current platforms overlook this individuality. Role Call uses AI to offer highly personalized recommendations, increasing the chances of finding the right roles faster.

No effective platform for showing actor portfolio

Casting platforms are only made for the actor searching for a role, yet there is no outlet for those searching for actors. It is unheard of to post a portfolio online, showcasing previous projects and talents that can be brought to the table. A resumé alone doesn't capture an actor’s presence, versatility, or emotional range. Videos, monologues, and headshots are vital but underutilized. Role Call allows users to build dynamic, media-rich portfolios that directors and companies can actively browse — reversing the search process and creating inbound opportunities.

Lack of industry connections

Similar to the film and TV industry, connections is everything. It is all about who you know. Without an agent, it is virtually impossible to find anything if one has no connections. Platforms have no outlet to connect with other actors and casting directors, there is no medium to bridge the gap between these faults. Most aspiring actors are excluded from industry networks, and existing platforms do little to create genuine connection. Role Call bridges this gap by offering features like direct messaging, industry matchmaking, and live audition networking events — helping actors build visibility and credibility from day one.

Solution

Online platform for networking and finding autitions

Role Call is a website designed for the theater lovers who want to connect, inspire, and perform. With this medium, it will be easier than ever to reach the audiences not only in the theater, but in the networking world. Actors will be able to directly message and comunicate with companies and casting directors, and the latter will be able to directly view actors' portfolios' and assess immediately. Getting rid of the middlemen in the typical casting process simplifies the process for everyone.     

Using AI to make each profile user-specific

With the advancements in AI, each user will see content and job opportunities catered to them and their innterests, boosting engagement and likelihood of returning and coninutation of the use of the website.

Ability to share resume pubicly with added pictures and videos

With the addidtion of adding photos and videos to a resume, for a casting director an actor will not only look good on paper, but the proof will be only a click away on the user's profile. Adding photos and videos from specific performances, similar to an athlete's highlight reel, will prove their talent and have a higher likelihood of building a network and furthering their resume with Role Call.

Customer segments

Theater lovers

• Drama students • Theatre clubs • Local theaters • amateur actors Role call is focused more on the amateur / start-up side of the theater industry, giving a boost to those who need it. When an actor is starting out, they may not know where to go or what to do. They may not have the means or resources to pay for an agent or connect with people higher up inthe food chain. Role call is accessible for everyone. Local theaters will be able to spread the word of their performances to a wider audience, attracting more people for auditions and creating a better show overall.

Ideal customer

'An aspiring actor or Drama student with strong interest in being a theatre actor/actress and building their resumé.'

The ideal customer for Role Call is an aspiring theater actor between the ages of 18 and 35 who is in the early stages of their career. They may have recently graduated from a performing arts program or are transitioning into theater from related fields such as dance or musical performance. This actor is highly motivated to grow their presence in the industry, regularly searches for auditions, and is eager to build a compelling portfolio. However, they often feel lost in traditional casting platforms, overwhelmed by irrelevant listings, and limited by static resumés that don’t fully showcase their talent. They typically don’t have an agent and are seeking direct access to casting opportunities. Tech-savvy and open to innovation, they value personalized tools that save time and help them stand out. Role Call offers exactly what they need: tailored audition recommendations powered by AI, a multimedia resumé builder to showcase their unique skills through photos and video clips, and a streamlined way to connect directly with theater companies and casting directors. For this customer, Role Call is more than a platform—it’s a launchpad for their acting career.                    

Unique value propositions

Role Call is a platform empowering aspiring theater actors to seamlessly connect with theaters, discover audition opportunities, and build their presence in the performing arts industry.

Unfair advandages

A networking website where opportunities are catered specifically to the user

Unfair advantage: Traditional casting platforms offer general listings. By leveraging AI to tailor opportunities based on a user’s skill set, interests, and experience, Role Call saves time and increases hit rates for auditions — something competitors likely don’t do as smartly or personally. Why it’s hard to replicate: Requires strong data science, AI models, and personalized recommendation logic that most generic platforms won’t have the resources or data to implement.

A direct line of access from actors to companies and theater groups

Unfair advantage: Bypasses middlemen like agents or casting directories. Direct access democratizes exposure and reduces gatekeeping — a unique draw for newer actors. Why it’s hard to replicate: Establishing trusted, two-way industry connections (between aspiring actors and legit theater groups) takes time, reputation, and trust — a big barrier for new entrants.
