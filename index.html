Who are we?

Welcome to ROLLCALL, the ultimate platform for drama students, theatre clubs, and local theatres!

Whether you're an aspiring actor, director, or playwright, this is the place to connect, grow, and find new opportunities. Create a profile, showcase your portfolio, and discover fellow theatre enthusiasts across the Netherlands. Find open castings, explore local theatres and drama clubs, and build your network—all in one place.

What do we stand for?

Opportunity for all

We believe that everyone deserves a chance to show who they are and what they are capable of. We break down barriers so actors can find opportunities regardless of background or experience.

Authentic connections

Talent is one of the keys for success, but is not everything. The right connections are what will bring you where you want to be. We create a space where the relationships between actors, casting professionals, and industry insiders can thrive.

Visibility and access

Access to tools, resources and a place to showcase your portfolio is what will allow actors to take charge of their careers and land the roles they always dreamed of.

Inclusivity and representation

Every actor deserves a chance to be seen and represented, despite their age, nationality or sex. We help everyone to get fair chances and equal treatment in the industry.
