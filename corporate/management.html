JUSTIFICATION OF LEAN CANVAS

Product

Rolecall is a creative hub built for the next generation of theatre makers in the Netherlands. Whether you're an actor, director, or writer, this is your stage to grow, connect, and be seen.

Problem

Difficult to find auditions in one place

Aspiring actors often struggle to find auditions, especially without an agent or agency. Most amateur actors have to rely on paid websites like Backstage to find roles, and even then, they may not be qualified, in the area, or it may not be a role that fits them.

Lack of personalized filters

One of the biggest issues that actors face is finding auditions, let alone booking the role. Pre-existing casting websites may only go as far as to specify your age and gender, barely focusing on the true needs and desires of those on the prowl for a job.

No effective platform for showing actor portfolio

Casting platforms are only made for the actor searching for a role, yet there is no outlet for those searching for actors. It is unheard of to post a portfolio online, showcasing previous projects and talents that can be brought to the table.

Lack of industry connections

Similar to the film and TV industry, connections is everything. It is all about who you know. Without an agent, it is virtually impossible to find anything if one has no connections. Platforms have no outlet to connect with other actors and casting directors, there is no medium to bridge the gap between these faults.

Solution

Online platform for networking and finding autitions

Role Call is a website designed for the theater lovers who want to connect, inspire, and perform. With this medium, it will be easier than ever to reach the audiences not only in the theater, but in the networking world. Actors will be able to directly message and comunicate with companies and casting directors, and the latter will be able to directly view actors' portfolios' and assess immediately.

Using AI to make each profile user-specific

With the advancements in AI, each user will see content and job opportunities catered to them and their innterests, boosting engagement and likelihood of returning and coninutation of the use of the website.

Ability to share resume pubicly with added pictures and videos

With the addidtion of adding photos and videos to a resume, for a casting director an actor will not only look good on paper, but the proof will be only a click away on the user's profile. Adding photos and videos from specific performances, similar to an athlete's highlight reel, will prove their talent and have a higher likelihood of building a network and furthering their resume with Role Call.

Customer segments

Theater lovers

• Drama students • Theatre clubs • Local theaters • amateur actors Role call is focused more on the amateur / start-up side of the theater industry, giving a boost to those who need it. When an actor is starting out, they may not know where to go or what to do. They may not have the means or resources to pay for an agent or connect with people higher up inthe food chain. Role call is accessible for everyone. Local theaters will be able to spread the word of their performances to a wider audience, attracting more people for auditions and creating a better show overall.

Ideal customer

'A Drama student with strong interest in being a theatre actor/actress and building their resumé.'

A student who has to juggle their time between studying and building their resumé may not have time to network constantly, so Role Call does it for them. Connecting with theater industry proffesionals and scheduling auditions is easier than ever on Role Call.                    

Unique value propositions

Role Call is a platform empowering aspiring theater actors to seamlessly connect with theaters, discover audition opportunities, and build their presence in the performing arts industry.
