Who are we?

Welcome to ROLLCALL, the ultimate platform for drama students, theatre clubs, and local theatres!

Whether you're an aspiring actor, director, or playwright, this is the place to connect, grow, and find new opportunities. Create a profile, showcase your portfolio, and discover fellow theatre enthusiasts across the Netherlands. Find open castings, explore local theatres and drama clubs, and build your network—all in one place.

Let’s bring the stage to life together!
